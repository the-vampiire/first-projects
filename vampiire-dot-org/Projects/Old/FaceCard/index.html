Welcome to FaceCard

A new mind-training tool to help you remember acquaintences and business associates.

  • In this fast paced world it is difficult to maintain a strong association between names and the faces they belong to.

  • FaceCard trains your mind to be a master of first impressions in two minutes a day.

  • Being able to pick an acquaintence out of a crowd and remember their name and information about them is a guaranteed way to increase your relationship with that person.
